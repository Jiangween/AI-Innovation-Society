About Us

The AI Innovation & Creation Society of Zhejiang Gongshang University is committed to promoting education in artificial intelligence and algorithms. Through competitions and research projects, the society nurtures students' creativity and practical abilities, helping them grow into future technology talents.

Learn More

Membership

The society welcomes students who are passionate about artificial intelligence and algorithm competitions. Members enjoy opportunities to participate in competitions, access academic resources, and engage in projects. At the same time, they are expected to take on responsibilities within the society to drive its growth.

Learn More

Organization Structure

The structure of the society includes:

  • Presidential Committee: Responsible for overall planning and external communications.
  • Department Heads: Includes the Competition, Public Relations, and Administration Departments, each handling specific functions.
  • Management Team: Composed of the Presidential Committee and Department Heads to oversee all activities.
Learn More

Election and Management Policies

The election process is as follows:

  • Election applications are conducted in the second semester of each academic year, with open elections for positions in the Presidential and Department Heads committees.
  • Applicants must submit an application form and participate in an interview evaluation.
  • The final list of elected members will be announced internally, followed by an election conference.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How can I join the AI Innovation Society?

You can join by filling out the registration form or signing up during the “Club Fair” event.

How does the society manage elections?

Elections are held in the second semester of each academic year. Applicants must submit an application form and attend an interview.

Does the society charge membership fees?

No, the society does not charge any membership fees. Activity funds are provided by the university.
