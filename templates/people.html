About Us

The AI Innovation Society (A&A Club) of Zhejiang Gongshang University, established in 2022 under the guidance of the School of Artificial Intelligence, aims to promote knowledge of AI and algorithms, nurture competitive talents, and organize various events to enrich students' extracurricular life.

Advisors

Dr. Jingjing Zhou

Associate Professor, School of Information and Electrical Engineering. She holds a master’s degree from Shandong University and a Ph.D. from the University of Science and Technology Beijing. Visiting scholar at the University of Florida.

Research: AI, Network Architectures, Network Traffic. Published 20+ papers, holds 4 national patents, and leads several key projects. Coach of the ACM Training Team.

Dr. Xudong Zhu

Professor, School of Management. Ph.D. from the Chinese Academy of Sciences. Visiting scholar at the University of Florida.

Research: Big Data, NLP, Storage Management. Published 30+ papers, holds 10+ patents. Coach of ACM Training Team and CCF NOI certified coach.

Dr. Wanying Chen

Associate Professor, School of Management. Degrees from INSA-Lyon and Northwestern Polytechnical University. Joint research with Laval University, Canada.

Research: Logistics, Automation, Operational Optimization. Recognized as "Qianjiang Talent" and "West Lake Scholar."
