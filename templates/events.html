Events & Competitions

The AI Innovation Society of Zhejiang Gongshang University actively participates in and organizes various AI and algorithm-related competitions, offering students opportunities to enhance their technical skills and foster innovation. Below are the major competitions we take part in or host:

Major Competitions

  • ICPC (International Collegiate Programming Contest) - Asia Regionals (Nanjing and other regions)
  • CCPC (China Collegiate Programming Contest) - National and Regional Events
  • Baidu Star Programming Contest
  • China Collegiate Computing Contest - Group Programming Ladder Tournament
  • Tusimple Cup - Zhejiang Collegiate Programming Contest
  • Global Campus AI Algorithm Elite Competition
  • Huawei Cup - China Graduate Mathematical Modeling Competition
  • SF Express Cup - Zhejiang Collegiate Logistics Design Contest
  • Bank of China Cup - Zhejiang Collegiate Career Planning Contest
  • Mathor-Cup - Collegiate Mathematical Modeling Challenge (Big Data Competition)

Club Flagship Events

In addition to participating in national and provincial competitions, the AI Innovation Society organizes the following flagship events on campus to cultivate students' practical abilities and innovative thinking:

  • University-level AI Competition
  • University-level Programming Contest

Significance of Competitions

By participating in these competitions, students not only improve their technical skills and gain valuable experience but also meet like-minded peers and broaden their horizons. Additionally, students with outstanding competition results may receive recommendations for postgraduate study or internship opportunities at renowned companies.
